Joy's easy banana bread

Recipe:

Joy's easy to prepare banana bread

like the title specifies this is a simple recipe to make banana bread, let's all thank Joy in our hearts, for this heartfealt recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup of melted butter
  • 1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan. I would personally skip the grease.
  2. Mash the 3 bananas,
  3. Add egg, sugar and butter.
  4. Mix the flour and baking soda together in a separate bowl.
  5. Stir into the banana mixter until the batter is just mixed
  6. Stir salt into batter.
  7. Pour batter into a prepared loaf pan
  8. bake into the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted in the middle of the centre comes out clean. whichs is for about an hour.
