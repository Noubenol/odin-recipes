Oven-baked Asparagus

Recipe:

Super easy, quick to prepare, oven roasted asparagus.

Simple dish that requieres to pre-heat the oven, First start by pre-heating the oven at 425°, wash the asparagus and trim the bottoms. then place the spears in a shallow oven proof dish, drizzle with 3 tablespoons of olive oil, toss to evenly coat, proceed to sprinkle with 1 1\2 tablespoons of grated parmesan cheese, add the minced garlic, add a tablespoon of salt, half teaspoon of ground black pepper. Toss spears to evenly coat, arrange the dish in a single line, and bake in the oven until the spears are tender, for 12 to 15 minutes depending on the thickness of the asparagus. drizzle the asparagus with a tablespoon of lemon juice. proceed to toss again, and finish with another sprinkle of parmessan cheese.

Ingredients:

  • Medium Garlic clove
  • Bunch of thin asparagus spears
  • Olive oil
  • Parmessan cheese
  • Ground black pepper
  • Sea salt
  • Olive oil

Steps:

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 425°
  2. Peel and mince a medium clove of garlic
  3. Wash one bunch of thin asparagus spears
  4. Trim the bottoms
  5. Place spears in a shallow oven proof dish
  6. Drizzle them with 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  7. Toss to evenly coat
  8. Sprinkle with 1 1\2 tablespoons of grated parmessan cheese
  9. Add the minced garlic
  10. Add a teaspoon of sea salt
  11. Add a half teaspoon of ground black pepper
  12. Toss spears again to evenly coat
  13. Arrange in a single layer
  14. Bake in oven until asparagus spears are just tender, 12 to 15 mins depending on asparagus thickness
  15. Drizzle asparagus with a tablespoon of lemon juice
  16. Toss Again
  17. Sprinkle some more parmessan
